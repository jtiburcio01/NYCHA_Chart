NYCHA-Contracted Exterminators Paid More as Rat Sightngs Increase

Payment gaps between NYCHA vendors hint at corruption as investigations reveal corruption elsewhere.

By Julian Tiburcio

April 12, 2024

Of all the exterminating companies contracted by NYCHA between 2016 and 2023, only four have been awarded over $1 million by the agency. Of these companies, only Royal Exterminating Co’s contract was obtained through a competitive bid.

Bug Queen, First Response Exterminating, and Kingsway Exterminating Co’s contracts were all awarded through sealed bids, a procurement method that is prone to corruption.

Rat sightings on NYCHA properties have increased since 2022, increasing the need for reliable pest control in public housing.
